Department Of CSE,CSBS & AIDS

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering was started in the year 2009 to render services to the student community to meet as the global industrial experts. The department is well equipped with state of the art laboratories with internet and Wi-Fi facility.

The objective of the department is to develop competent professionals with innovative research capabilities. The students must to do a project on or off-campus in the final year with an option to choose their platform and thus they are exposed to the real time computer problems which will mold them to conquer the challenging IT world.


Jfinagles

“JFINAGLES” is a national level technical symposium organized by the JCT College of Engineering and Technology,Pichanur, Coimbatore. The Symposium provides an opportunity to share innovative views and ideas on latest and emerging trends in technology. Jfinagles 23 is a much awaited technical festival that is tailor made to help future professionals to enhance their technical and interpersonal skills. The Symposium provides scope for kindling minds of the students and updating their knowledge, thereby preparing them for stepping into august industries employing cutting edge technologies. Events at Jfinagles are not just renowned for the talented participants and passionate minds but also for the technical excellence and professional manner in which they are conducted.
